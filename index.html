FAQ

How much does it cost?

We don’t know yet! We’re providing free setup and free trial for select awesome organizations while we work out pricing. Contact us!

Will there be a free version?

We hope to provide a free version to ad hoc organizations with' near-zero budgets, yes!

What's the delivery rate?

Right now it’s 70%, which is actually better than any comparable option. We think we can get it to 80% within coming months, and ultimately to 95% and beyond.

Is it just for US Congress? What about state government? Or other countries? Or agencies?

We're happy to discuss solutions for other institutions including outside the US. Be in touch!

What's the project based on? Is it open source?

The product is based on work by Sina Khanifar, EFF, and volunteers from around the world, with our own modifications for making it easy to use on typical petition pages.

About us

Fight for the Future is the nonprofit Internet Freedom activism group best known for its work defeating SOPA/PIPA and advancing Net Neutrality (Open Internet Rules) in the United States.

We're providing the best available methods for contacting Congress (methods that we've used for multiple winning campaigns) as a paid service.

To learn more about our work, visit: fightforthefuture.org
